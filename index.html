Our Mission

Empowering the next generation of problem solvers at Glen Allen High School.

The GAHS Math Olympiad club is a community of dedicated students who share a passion for mathematical theory, logic, and competitive problem-solving. We foster an environment where complex challenges are met with collaborative ingenuity.
